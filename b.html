Lucky Cat

Rub on the Lucky Cat to get a lucky number!


The lucky cat acts as a good luck charm for this website. The oval shaped object that it is holding is called a Koban. It is a gold coin used during the Edo period of feudal Japan. The characters on the coin read "Sen Man Ryo" which means 10 million Ryo. 1 Ryo was considered equivalent to the amount of rice needed to feed a person for a year. If you would like to know more about Lucky Cats in general, you should give Lucky Maneki Neko a visit.


Instead of some phony pseudo-random number generator, the number generator implemented for the Lucky Cat is a high quality true random number generator based on chaotic tent maps as described by Zhou et al. in this journal article.
